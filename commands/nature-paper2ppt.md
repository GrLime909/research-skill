Trigger the `nature-paper2ppt` skill. Identifies the paper type and argument, selects only the figures needed for the story, writes Chinese slide content and speaker notes, creates the actual .pptx deck, and runs an explicit self-review/corrective revision loop focused on figure quality, text overflow prevention, and non-template visual design. For 论文做PPT, 组会PPT, 文献汇报, 学术汇报.
