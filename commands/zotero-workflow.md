Trigger the `zotero-workflow` skill. Three modes: (1) `single` — read one paper deeply and write a structured Obsidian note with frontmatter; (2) `batch` — batch-process all unprocessed items in a Zotero collection with breakpoint-resume; (3) `query` — semantic search and retrieval from the Zotero library (no note written). Requires zotero-mcp and Obsidian MCP.
